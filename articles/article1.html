Halloweek Preparation

October 24, 2025

As Halloweek approaches, it’s time to get ready for the spooky celebrations in Torn City. Here are some tips to help you prepare:

Getting a Basket:

First, you’ll need to purchase a basket from Sally’s Sweet Shop in the city for $35. The shop restocks 1,000 baskets every five minutes, so if it’s sold out, just wait a bit and refresh until more are available.

Earning Treats:

Attacking any player can reward you with treats. The default chance to loot candy from your first basket is quite low, but this improves with basket upgrades. You can also boost your chances by wearing Spooky clothing and landing the finishing hit using a Spooky weapon—provided you’ve unlocked the I See Dead People and Here’s Johnny! basket perks.

Leveling Up Your Basket:

Your basket levels up automatically after collecting enough treats. The upgrade happens the next time you earn a treat from an attack. Treats aren’t consumed in this process. Each level increases your candy drop chance by 5%, up to a maximum of +45% at the Nightmarish level.

Upgrading Your Basket:

Basket upgrades unlock additional perks, such as higher drop rates and better candy rewards. These upgrades do consume your collected treats. To upgrade, go to your Special Items inventory and click “Use Item” next to your basket.
